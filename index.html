About Me

Manubhav Tamta is a budding actor who believes in living the moment as always , he is fun loving , making everyone laugh, hardworking , one you wanna be friend with and serious to his goal kind of person . Manubhav has shown his interest in acting since early age of 10 , since that he hasn't stopped and has performed in various theatre as well as street shows , born and brought up in the capital of worlds 2nd largest populated country 'INDIA'. Manubhav has always shown the ability of standing out of crowd and making long lasting impression on everyone in the room. Manubhav move to U.S. in his teenage years with his family for good and since then has been always supported by his peers and teachers there to make his career in this field, currently beside from making his portfolio and gaining acting experience in acting he is pursuing his associate degree in COMPsc(H).
